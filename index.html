Biography

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Storage Systems Group group at IBM Research Almaden which I joined in December 2016. Previously, I obtained a PhD degree from Imperial College London where I was part of the LSDS Group. My supervisor was Prof. Peter Pietzuch. I hold a MSc and BSc degree in Computer Science from Technical University Munich (TUM) where I worked under Prof. Alfons Kemper on P2P-based data management systems.
