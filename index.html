Researcher, Storage Systems Group

San Jose, CA, USA
Lukas.Rupprecht (at) ibm.com

I am a Research Staff Member in the Storage Systems Group group at IBM Research Almaden which I joined in December 2016 as a Postdoc. Previously, I received a PhD degree from Imperial College London where I was part of the LSDS Group. My supervisor was Prof. Peter Pietzuch. I hold a MSc and BSc degree in Computer Science from Technical University Munich (TUM) where I worked under Prof. Alfons Kemper on P2P-based data management systems.
